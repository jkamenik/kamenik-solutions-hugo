**Code** is the garden quadrant for **dependencies and notations in the repo**: languages, libraries, frameworks, and test harnesses you import or reference. It is not where we classify things you **operate** (**[[Tool]]**), **deploy on** (**[[Platform]]**), or **how you work** (**[[Technique]]**).
