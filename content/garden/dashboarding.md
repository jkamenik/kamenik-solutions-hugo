Under **[[Tool]]**, **Dashboarding** groups products that **visualize queried data in shareable dashboards**. This is the BI and observability UI layer, not the warehouse, ETL, metrics pipeline, or polling/alerting platform underneath. For the latter, see **[[Monitoring]]**.
