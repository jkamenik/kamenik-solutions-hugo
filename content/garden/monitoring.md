Under **[[Tool]]**, **Monitoring** groups products that **watch infrastructure or services and raise alerts** when checks fail or thresholds breach. This is the collect-and-notify layer. **[[Dashboarding]]** is the visualize-and-share layer. **[[OpenTelemetry]]** is the telemetry standard and SDK layer under **[[Code]]**.
