Containerization is the act of encapsulating a runtime in an OCI format. This makes it portable to any platform that supports OCI. {{% wl "Docker" %}} is the birth of containerization and remains very popular, but it is by no means the only solution. Regardless of which tooling you choose you will be well served by containerization.
