[Kubecost](https://www.kubecost.com/) is IBM's commercial Kubernetes cost monitoring and optimization platform. It extends the OpenCost lineage with rightsizing, alerts, governance, and optional SaaS management. We **assess** it when teams need enterprise FinOps workflows beyond what the OSS **[[OpenCost]]** stack provides out of the box.
